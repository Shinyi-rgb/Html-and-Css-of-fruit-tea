DIY Fruit Tea

Pure natural ingredients show the greatest benefits of fruit tea to the human body

Brewing tea

If you simply soak it, the taste of the fruit tea will inevitably be a little lighter. If you want a stronger taste, you can choose to make your own fruit tea by boiling it. First, cut the prepared fruit into pieces and put them into water. You can brew a single fruit or a mixture of fruits. Then decide whether to add sugar or tea leaves according to your personal preference. Boil the water until it boils, and turn off the heat after a few minutes. Whether it is hot or with ice cubes, it is a delicious and healthy homemade fruit tea!

Brewing

This is a relatively simple way to make your own fruit tea at home. Simply soak your favorite fruit or dried fruit in water and let it sit for a while. When the flavor of the fruit is absorbed into the water, your homemade fruit tea is ready! If you want a stronger flavor, you can also choose to refrigerate it for 8 to 10 hours after soaking or add juice. The final fruit tea will be even more delicious!

Jam for tea

In addition to making fruit tea with fresh or dried fruit, more and more people are choosing to make fruit tea with jam! Currently, many local businesses and small farmers are promoting natural products and ingredients to make natural jams that have a wide range of uses. They can be added to water to make fruit tea, or mixed into yogurt or food to add flavor. Please note that only jams made from natural fruits are suitable for making fruit tea. If it is a cheap jam with too many additives or spices, the taste of the fruit tea will be far from what you expect.
